About Us

Theo’s Detailing is a two-man mobile detailing operation serving the Raleigh-Durham area. We’re built on one simple goal: to provide the absolute best quality.

We officially launched in August 2025 with a focus on precision, professionalism, and consistency. Every vehicle gets our full attention, using trusted tools and techniques to deliver results that speak for themselves.

We come to you, work with care, and leave your car looking its best. That’s the standard. Nothing less.
